Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,Cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with thise festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart crambererries, and cteamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups(500g) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups(350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cupextra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though is not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cramberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-size mixing bowl, or in a standard mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together until the flour id fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
